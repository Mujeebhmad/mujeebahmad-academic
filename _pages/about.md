## Welcome

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Institute of Physics, Polish Academy of Sciences (IF PAN), Warsaw, Poland.

My research focuses on superconducting quantum devices, Josephson junctions, quantum transport, topological quantum materials, and thermoelectric phenomena.

I combine advanced materials synthesis, nanofabrication, and cryogenic measurements to investigate fundamental quantum phenomena and develop next-generation quantum technologies.

### Research Interests

- Superconducting Quantum Devices
- Quantum Transport
- Topological Quantum Materials
- Thermoelectric Materials
- Low Temperature Physics
- Nanofabrication
